      'We Rate Dogs' is, a twitter account that rates dogs based on how cute and how helpful they are, but only dog pets are allowed, which is not always the case. The dogs are rated out of 10, however the ratings of most dogs are above 10, because why not!

      Analysis¶


      On this discovery, my data analysis instincts kicked in and I wanted to know which of the dog stage is rated highest and which one is rated lowest. This could come in handy especially when you want to invest in a life-long best friend.
      For the anlysis of this database I used Python because of its ease and availability of useful libraries such as;

      1. Pandas
      2. Numpy
      3. Requests
      4. Tweepy
      5. Json.

      After analysis, I discovered very instresting insights,

      1. All numeric variables are skewed to the right, meaning the least values have occurred the most.
      2. We have conf. = 100%, it must be clear picture for a specific breed of dog.
      3. The lowest dog rating_numerator is 0 rating and the highest is 1776 very interesting
